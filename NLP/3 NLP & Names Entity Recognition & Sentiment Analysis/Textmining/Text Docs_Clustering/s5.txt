A half century from Haris Sohail helped the Sialkot Stallions end their t20 Champions League campaign with a consolation sic wicket victory over the Hampshire Royals at the Wanderers in Johannesburg.

With both sides already knocked out of the tournament Sohail smashed 63 runs which included eight boundaries and one six to help the Pakistan t20 champions chase down the Royals total of 143-8 with five balls to spare.

The days other game between Trinidad and Tobago and Uva Next was abandoned after one ball of Uva Next’s run chase after heavy rain forced the players with Uva Next 0-1 to end their chances of chasing down a victory target of 181-3
